About Me

Hi! My name is Poppy, and I'm a voice actor based in the UK! Alongside voice acting, some of my greatest passions include character concept art, singing, content creation and skateboarding.

I've been voice acting for roughly thirteen years now, and I've always known it was my calling. I love how it makes me feel, the joy it brings me and how well I can tell a story. Voice acting was a passion raised to me after one of my favourite video games began openly casting for roles. I submitted an audition expecting nothing back. I now voice my favourite character in the game, which is one of my most proudest achievements to date.

It helped me realise that nothing is off limits for me if I really set my mind to it. That's how voice acting became the biggest love in my life. I'm surrounded by the most wonderful and supportive friends in the world; named Mark, Millie and Sterre. They have helped me push forward through the tough moments both imposter syndrome and self doubt have brought me with this career, and I now feel nothing but excitement when being met with new opportunities. Not to mention my adoptive father, who is not only a voice actor, but my mentor. I would be nowhere near where I am today without his guidance!
